Introduction

Product Designer · Full Stack Developer. A native iOS application designed to help freelancers and creatives manage income and expenses with clarity, speed, and minimal friction. Freelancers often face irregular income and complex financial tracking, yet most finance apps are designed for traditional employees. Existing solutions felt generic, overwhelming, and stressful to use.

Process

The project began with research and discovery through interviews with freelancers and independent workers, combined with an analysis of existing personal finance apps to identify usability gaps and common pain points.

Based on these insights, wireframes were created with a strong focus on speed and simplicity. The interface was then developed into high-fidelity designs in Figma, aligned with Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines, and iterated continuously to remove unnecessary steps.

The application was built using Swift and SwiftUI, with Firebase integrated for authentication and data storage. Core user flows were tested directly on real devices throughout development to ensure reliability and performance.

Design Decisions & Outcome

A minimalist interface was prioritized to reduce financial anxiety and improve clarity. Visual summaries replaced dense tables, native iOS patterns were followed to shorten the learning curve, and user flows were designed to require as little manual input as possible.

The result was a polished and functional iOS application. Early feedback highlighted its clarity, ease of use, and the value of simplicity when working with personal finance tools.
