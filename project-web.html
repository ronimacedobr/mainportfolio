Logistics Web

Introduction

A modern marketing website designed for a logistics and freight company, focused on clarity, credibility, and operational scale. Logistics platforms often struggle to communicate complex services in a simple and trustworthy way.

Process

The project began with an analysis of logistics and freight industry websites to identify common usability and communication issues, including dense layouts, unclear messaging, and lack of visual trust signals.

Based on these insights, the information architecture was restructured to prioritize clarity and flow. Wireframes defined a clear hierarchy between services, company positioning, and calls to action. High-fidelity designs were developed in Webflow.

Particular attention was given to responsive behavior, ensuring the site remained clear and readable across devices. Clean typography, restrained color usage, and strong visual balance convey professionalism and scale.

Design Decisions & Outcome

The information architecture was structured to prioritize clarity and flow. Wireframes defined a clear hierarchy between services, company positioning, and calls to action. High-fidelity designs focused on clean typography and strong visual balance.

The final result is a streamlined, modern logistics website that presents complex services in a clear, confident, and accessible way across all devices.
