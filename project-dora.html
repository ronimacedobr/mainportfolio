Dora Legal SaaS

Introduction

Dora is a Legal Practice Management SaaS designed and built end-to-end for the Brazilian legal market. The platform orchestrates clients, cases, finance, subscriptions, and AI‑assisted workflows with a real-world focus on legal ops.

Year
2025
Industry

Scope of work
Product Design & Full-stack Architecture
Timeline
2026

Process

The project started with product definition and UX design in Figma, translating legal workflows into task-oriented interfaces that support daily professional use.

A modular frontend architecture was implemented using Next.js App Router, React, and TypeScript, with reusable components for data-heavy dashboards.

Backend services were built on Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, RLS) and Stripe for subscriptions, with middleware-based session handling, validation, and entitlements wired end-to-end.

Design Decisions & Outcome

The design prioritizes clarity, compliance, and speed—legal dashboards surface the right context without overwhelming the user, and micro-interactions reinforce trust for high-stakes work.

Outcomes include a production-grade SaaS architecture, polished UX, and automated workflows that respect legal data sensitivity while staying fast and intuitive for practice managers.
